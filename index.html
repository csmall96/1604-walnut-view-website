What Makes This Home Special

Spa-Inspired Retreat

The beautifully remodeled bathroom features a dual-sink vanity with marble countertop, sage green tile, gold fixtures, and a spacious walk-in shower with waterfall showerhead. Built-in shelving adds both style and storage.

Cozy by Design

The spacious primary suite features a dramatic custom accent wall, warm hardwood floors, and gold wall sconces that create an inviting atmosphere. Natural light pours in through large windows overlooking the neighborhood.

Versatile Flex Space

The lower level features a Murphy bed that transforms from home office to guest bedroom in seconds. Perfect for remote work, visiting friends, or as a rental unit with its own separate entrance.

Seamless Living

The open-concept main level connects kitchen, dining, and living areas for effortless entertaining. A statement sputnik chandelier, sage green wainscoting, and hardwood floors create a modern yet warm atmosphere.

Built-in Organization

Custom closet systems throughout the home maximize storage with designated spaces for everything. Multiple hanging rods, drawers, and shelving keep your wardrobe organized and accessible.

Protected Parking

The attached single-car garage provides secure, covered parking plus additional storage space. Direct entry into the home means no weather worries, and there's room for bikes, gear, and seasonal items.

Full-Size Laundry Room

A dedicated laundry room with full-size Samsung washer and dryer gives you the capacity to tackle big loads. Overhead storage keeps supplies organized, while natural light from a large window brightens the space.

Your Outdoor Escape

The private covered balcony is perfect for morning coffee or evening relaxation. String lights add ambiance while the covered design means you can enjoy it rain or shine. Views of mature trees and the quiet neighborhood.

Connected to the City

Just minutes from the LYNX Gold Line, you'll have easy access to Uptown Charlotte, NoDa, and beyond. Skip the traffic and parking hassles while enjoying all the city has to offer.

Historic Charm, Modern Living

Wesley Heights is one of Charlotte's most walkable and historic neighborhoods. Tree-lined streets, friendly neighbors, and easy access to local coffee shops, restaurants, and parks make this the perfect place to call home.
